  We introduce a synthetic ground-truth approach for drusen segmentation in OCT, combining sinusoidal image deformations of healthy retinal scans with a generative adversarial network to produce paired image-mask training data without manual annotation. The method enables robust drusen detection while sidestepping the cost and variability of expert pixel-level labeling.
